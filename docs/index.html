Common Questions

Is this actually free? What's the catch?
Completely free. No email required. No paywall on results. We built this as a resource for operators because the restaurant industry needs better diagnostic tools, not more generic advice. If you want deeper help after seeing your results, there is an option for that, but the diagnostic stands on its own.
How accurate is a 10-question diagnostic?
It identifies directional signal, not decimal-point precision. Think of it like a doctor's initial screening: it narrows the field so you know where to look deeper. Operators who have used it consistently report that the primary bottleneck it identifies matches their intuition, but gives them language and sequence they were missing.
What if my results show multiple bottlenecks?
That is a real and common result. It means your restaurant has multiple constraints competing for attention. The diagnostic helps even here because it tells you which constraints scored highest and the recommended sequence for addressing them. Fixing things in the right order matters more than fixing everything at once.
Who built this? What is it based on?
This diagnostic is based on the Restaurant Money Model, a system that maps how restaurants generate profit through six connected stages: Demand, Capacity, Experience, Behavior, Menu Economics, and Profit. Each question in the diagnostic maps directly to one or more of these stages.
What is the Peak Profit Audit mentioned in the results?
It is a deeper, hands-on diagnostic for restaurant operators who want a full analysis of their specific numbers, staffing, and menu. It is a separate paid service. The diagnostic you just took is free, complete, and useful on its own. The audit is only for operators who want the full deep-dive.
Can I retake the diagnostic?
Yes. Refresh the page and start over. Your answers are not stored or saved anywhere. Everything runs in your browser.
